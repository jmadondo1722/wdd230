Overview

  • Who: Members of a Chamber of Commerce are usually international and local operating companies, such as lawyers, property developers, tourism companies, airlines, manufacturing companies, import and export businesses, banks, finance companies, legal advisors, IT and electronics manufacturers etc. Pretoria being the capital city would also attract a lot of up and coming companies.
  • Age: 18+.
  • Technology: The majority of the audience will access the site through mobile devices like cell phones, laptops and i-pads, the aged and senior citizens will probably use home computers most of the time.
  • Income: Income varies according to age and levels of education (Ranging between R 60 000 and R250 000 annually.).
  • Motivation: Chambers of Commerce main activities are, among others, safeguarding business interests, creating business interests and sharing business experiences and business interests, contact with governments, civil society, local media and the press and organizing trade shows and events.

Scenarios

  1. A young university graduate looking to start his own business and not certain where or how to start, what kind of information he might need to start.
  2. An investor looking for businesses to investing and all the relevant information associated with investing in businesses.
  3. The small business owner looking for some potential investors into his struggling business.
  4. The corporate executive looking to find and build networks.
  5. The public walks in for general information about businesses available in their city.
